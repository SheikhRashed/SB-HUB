Working Remotely

The world has become increasingly global and connected through technology.

Employees can now work from various locations, while still adding value and connecting with each other, our clients and stakeholders.

This site aims to provide you with all the information, tools and tips to decide whether you would like to work remotely, and how to get set up when you are approved to do so.

What is this ?

The remote working framework is aimed at providing flexible opportunities, where operationally possible, for employees to fulfil both their work and career aspirations, blending their responsibilities in a manner that suits their unique needs.

What is the bank doing this ?

The bank seeks to embrace workplace agility, while ensuring a balance between client, internal work operational requirements and employee enabling requirements.

How does it work?

For the purposes of this framework, remote working refers to an employee of the bank performing work during ordinary business hours, from a primary location which is not designated as a bank premises. For more detailed info, familiarise yourself with the relevant policy for working remotely:

  • Group
  • South Africa
Who is eligible?

As a leading financial institution, it is important that we maintain our clients’ confidence in our drive, passion and expertise. Consequently, it will not be feasible to provide remote working opportunities for every employee and every job. Click through to read more on the factors that determine whether your role is eligible for a remote working opportunity.
